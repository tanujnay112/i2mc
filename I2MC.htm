Interlake Invitational Math Competition

April 1st | 2:00pm to 8:30pm

Interlake High School


Register

How much is registration?
$10 per person
How large are teams?
A team should have three or four members
Who can come?
Open to all students who haven't graduated high school
How difficult are the problems?
AMC10/12 to AIME level

SCHEDULE

1:30pm-2:00pm | Registration

Interlake commons, come through the front entrance off NE 24th street

2:00-2:20 | Introduction and settling down

Find testing locations

2:25-3:05 | Individual Test: geometry/combinatorics

10 problems in 40 minutes

3:10-3:50 | Individual Test: algebra/number theory

10 problems in 40 minutes

4:00-4:40 | Team test

12 problems in 40 minutes, teams submit their answers on a single answer sheet. Problems come from a variety of subjects.

4:50-6:00 | Guts

24 problems divided into sets of four. Turn in one set before you get the next. This test is scored immediately, and scores are publicly posted in real time. More information and sample problems here

6:00-6:50 | Dinner

We'll offer pepperoni, cheese, and Hawaiian pizza
Crossroads mall also has great options. Let us know if you have any dietary restrictions

6:50-7:30 | Individual Finals

Top 15 students complete 6 additional problems in 40 minutes. Ties will be broken by performance on the individual tests.

6:50-7:30 | Mini-events

Non-finalists (who are all still winners) can participate in one of three optional events
Estimathon: give ranges of numbers as answers to estimation problems. For example, how many people ride the London Underground each day?
Plank Countdown: Mathcounts style countdown tournament, but participants must plank
IMO true/false: answer true or false to some of the hardest proof problems we can find, largely from the International Mathematical Olympiad

8:00-8:30 | Awards Ceremony and Plank Countdown finals

Teams will be ranked based on the results of the individual, team, and guts rounsd. We will also crown the Ultimate Fitness Champion. Winner gets a potentially figurative olive branch.
